Typically Dutch
On the 5th of December, Dutch people traditionally celebrate the birthday of Sinterklaas, a legendary figure based on Saint Nicholas, the patron saint of children. On the evening of his birthday he traditionally passes by all homes to give presents and sweets to nice children.
On this website you will find recipes of some typical Dutch Sinterklaas candy.
